Scaled Biolabs

Scaled Biolabs is pioneering a Cell Therapy Discovery Engine, working at the frontier of microfluidics, automation, and machine learning to develop best-in-class cell-based medicines. This engine enables leading biotech and pharma companies to survey hundreds of parameters across myriad experimental processes, providing an unprecedented window into cell-driven biology. Scaled Biolabs operates customer-funded and internal programs, including a CIRM-funded program focusing on iPSC-derived dopaminergic neurons as a curative cell therapy for Parkinson’s disease. We are an early-stage, revenue-positive, Indie Bio graduate company, and co-located with many other dynamic companies at the MBC Biolabs incubator in the Dogpatch neighborhood of San Francisco.

Our Mission & Culture

Scaled Biolabs believes that multidisciplinary approaches to tough problems will allow us to sidestep the drawbacks of traditional biotechnology and allow us to realize the benefits of science that’s powerful, but hard to deliver. Our team combines biologists (wetware), engineers (hardware) and data scientists (software) to tackle meaningful frontiers such as cell therapies and regenerative medicine. We value empowering diverse talent to realize their best self, and achieve meaningful goals. We provide a stimulating, collaborative environment that promotes this by collective intercommunication.
